pre-order

Using this form you can pre-order a lil prah. There will be a limited amount of 200 boards. This is a one time project only, there are no second chances. The board will have your name on it so double check for typo's. If you don't include a name, we will leave it blank. If you want more than one board, you can fill out the form again. For now you will only need to indicate in what part of the world you live (where the board should be shipped to). When we are ready to produce the boards, you will receive a payment link and you will be asked for full shipping details. Shipping costs will be calculated during checkout. Only when you have completed the payment will your order be final.
